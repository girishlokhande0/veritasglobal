Dasheri Mango

Renowned as the King of Mangoes, Dasheri Mangoes are prized for their rich aroma, vibrant golden color, and naturally sweet flavor. Grown in the Konkan region of Maharashtra, India, these premium mangoes are carefully handpicked and exported worldwide, ensuring top quality and freshness in every shipment.

Details

  • Product : Mangoes (Seasonal Export – April to July)
  • Varieties : Dasheri Mango
  • Packaging : 6, 9, 10, 12, 15, 16, 18, 20 pieces per Carton Box (with protective netting)
  • Shelf Life : 10–15 days
  • Export Standards : Hot Water Treatment (HWT), APEDA Certified
  • Specialty : Naturally ripened, high sweetness
