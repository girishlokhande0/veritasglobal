FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our import-export services? We’ve compiled answers to the most common queries for your convenience.

  • What products do you export?

    We export premium Indian rice (basmati & non-basmati), fresh seasonal fruits, and a wide variety of vegetables to international markets.

  • Which countries do you export to?

    We export to countries across Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and North America. Our logistics partners ensure global reach and timely delivery.

  • How do you ensure the quality of your products?

    We follow strict quality control standards from sourcing to packaging. All products are hygienically handled and certified for international export.

  • Can I request a custom order or packaging?

    Yes, we offer custom orders and packaging solutions based on your business requirements. Reach out to us for a personalized quote.

  • What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

    MOQ varies by product type and destination. Typically, our MOQ starts from 1 container (20 ft) or as per mutual agreement for bulk supply.

  • What certifications do your products have?

    Our products comply with FSSAI, APEDA, and other relevant export certifications. We also provide phytosanitary and lab reports on request.

  • How long does shipping usually take?

    Shipping time depends on the destination and shipping method. On average, international shipments take 2–4 weeks from dispatch.

  • Do you provide samples before bulk orders?

    Yes, we offer samples for evaluation. Shipping charges may apply, depending on the destination and quantity requested.

  • How can I place an order?

    You can place an order by contacting us via email, phone, or through our website contact form. We'll guide you through every step.

  • Do you handle logistics and documentation?

    Absolutely. We manage everything from packing, freight, customs clearance, and export documentation to ensure a smooth and hassle-free process.
